Hello everyone! My name is Alex, and I am 20 years old. I am originally from Bolivia, but for the past eight months, I have been living in the beautiful city of Buenos Aires, Argentina.Being here is incredibly special to me. I fell deeply in love with this country and its people because I previously served a mission here. That experience left a lasting impression on my heart, which is exactly why I decided to come back and make this city my current home.On the academic side, I am focused on my personal and professional growth. I am currently pursuing my higher education online through the BYU-Pathway Worldwide program, which gives me the flexibility to study while adapting to my new life abroad.Additionally, I am filled with joy and gratitude to share some wonderful news about my home country: a new temple will soon be built in Bolivia. This is a historic and deeply meaningful milestone for my community and me, and I am truly excited for what the future holds!
